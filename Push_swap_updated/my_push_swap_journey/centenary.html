Agriculture Production Loan

Loan designed to finance business activities in the agricultural production, processing and marketing value chains.

Apply For Loan

Overview

Loan designed to finance business activities in the agricultural production, processing and marketing value chains, animal production (diary, poultry and piggery projects), fishing and fish farming, bee keeping as well as food processing i.e. grain mills, oil mills and hullers. The loan period and repayment plan is dependent on the nature and season of the agricultural activity to be financed.

Product Features

Loan period for working capital purpose is up to a maximum 24 months but development/ investment may exceed 24 months.

Experience in project being undertaken

Applicant must be resident within the branch designated area of operation

Applicant must be owner or tenant of the land on which he/she operates.
